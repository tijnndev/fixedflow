# FixedFlow - Feature Suggestions & Improvements

## 🎯 High Priority Features

### 1. **Payment Notifications & Reminders**
- Push notifications before payment due dates (1 day, 3 days, 1 week before)
- Customizable reminder preferences per payment
- Option to mark payments as "paid" to dismiss notifications
- Snooze functionality for reminders

### 2. **Payment History & Tracking**
- Mark individual occurrences as "paid" or "skipped"
- View payment history with filters (month, year, category)
- Show payment status indicators (upcoming, overdue, paid)
- Payment completion percentage for the month

### 3. **Analytics & Insights**
- Monthly/yearly spending trends chart
- Category breakdown pie chart
- Compare spending across different time periods
- Export reports as PDF or CSV
- Average monthly spending calculation

### 4. **Budget Management**
- Set monthly budget limits per category
- Overall monthly budget tracker
- Budget vs actual spending comparison
- Alerts when approaching or exceeding budget

### 5. **Payment Calendar Enhancements**
- Week view option
- Year overview with monthly totals
- Color coding by category
- Mini calendar widget on home screen
- Holiday/weekend indicators

## 🚀 Medium Priority Features

### 6. **Cloud Sync & Backup**
- Optional cloud backup (Google Drive, iCloud, Dropbox)
- Multi-device synchronization
- Export/import data functionality
- Automatic backup schedule

### 7. **Payment Templates**
- Save frequently used payment configurations
- Quick add from templates
- Template categories (utilities, subscriptions, loans, etc.)

### 8. **Advanced Recurrence Options**
- Every X weeks/months
- Bi-weekly payments
- Quarterly, semi-annual payments
- Custom recurrence patterns (e.g., "last Friday of month")
- End date for recurring payments

### 9. **Currency Support**
- ~~Multi-currency support~~
- Currency conversion
- Default currency setting per payment
- Exchange rate display

### 10. **Search & Filter**
- Global search for payments
- Filter by multiple criteria (category, amount range, date range)
- Sort options (amount, date, name, category)
- Saved filter presets
